Mishupy Pal

Vocalist/ Musician/ Drum Circle Facilitator /Special Educator                                            

Mishupy is a Certified Drum Circle Facilitator through Taal Inc and has been teaching music and facilitating Drum Circles in various schools and facilities.. A Sangeet Visharad in Indian classical music Vocals, from Akhil Bhartiya Gandharva Mahavidyalaya Mumbai and has also learnt the piano and successfully completed Intermidiate level exam from the Trinity College of London.

Mishupy is the Founder of Mishragati School of Music and has been conducting individual coaching sessions for people of all age groups; from various walks of life,since 2002. She also plays and teaches instruments like the Harmonium, Electronic Keyboard, Piano, Tanpura, Kartal,(Wooden Clappers), Cymbal, Conch (Shankh). Her unique methodologies have also helped her participants cope with various emotional, mental and physical discomforts; enabling them to overcome depression and anxiety through music. She has also made her mark with children with special needs and has facilitated healing through music.

Having a flare for music from a very young age; Mishupy has performed with various professional groups since childhood.

 Where words fail, music speaks. - Hans Christian Andersen

Mishupy believes that the Drum Circle benefits individuals by connecting them to a common purpose, thus enhancing their ability of creative self-expression.

The drum circle is also a springboard to bring people together for various reasons; ranging from team building, community development, creating an inclusive environment, personal healing, finding a connection with something deeper, a mode of self-expression, stress release, or even pure entertainment.
